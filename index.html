Frequently Asked Questions
How long does business registration take in Guyana? expand_more
TIN registration typically takes 2–3 business days. Company registration with the Deeds Registry takes 3–5 days. NIS registration is processed within 5 business days. When bundled, we aim for 5 business days total turnaround.
What documents do I need to register a business? expand_more
You'll need a valid National ID or Passport, proof of address, proposed business name(s), and details of business structure (sole trader, partnership, or company). We'll guide you through exactly what's needed for your specific case.
Can I register a business online? expand_more
Yes — BizHubGY handles the entire process digitally. Submit your information through our platform, and our compliance team manages all submissions with GRA, NIS, and the Deeds Registry on your behalf.
Do I need NIS registration? expand_more
If you have employees, NIS registration is mandatory. Even as a sole proprietor, registering for NIS provides access to benefits including old-age pension, sickness benefit, and maternity benefit. We recommend all business owners register.
What's the Local Content Certificate? expand_more
The Local Content Certificate is required for any business seeking contracts in Guyana's oil and gas sector. With 36,000+ new businesses registered in 2025 and the sector expanding rapidly, demand for certification is growing fast.
